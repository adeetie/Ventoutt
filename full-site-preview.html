Frequently Asked Questions

Is venting safe?

Yes, venting is safe. We prioritize your privacy and anonymity.

Who are your coaches?

Our coaches are trained professionals with experience in various fields of mental health.

Is this therapy?

No, venting and coaching are not replacements for clinical therapy, but they can be a great first step.

Will my chats stay private?

Absolutely. All chats are confidential and anonymous.

Are your psychologists licensed?

Yes, all our therapists and psychologists are licensed professionals.

Can I stay anonymous?

Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous during your sessions.
