Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ventoutt.

Is Ventoutt really anonymous? +
Yes! We prioritize your privacy above all else. You don't need to use your real name, and we do not track your personal conversations. Ventoutt is a safe space to be yourself.
Is it free to use? +
Absolutely. Joining the community, reading resources, and participating in public forums is completely free. We believe mental health support should be accessible to everyone.
Are there professionals on the platform? +
While Ventoutt is primarily a peer support community, we do provide resources curated by professionals. However, for crisis situations or therapy, we strongly recommend seeking licensed professionals.
How can I help others? +
You can support others by listening, sharing your own experiences, and offering kind words in our community forums. Remember, sometimes just being there is enough.
